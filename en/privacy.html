2. Collection and Storage of Personal Data

When you visit our website, information is automatically sent to our server by your browser. This information is temporarily stored in a log file. The following information is collected without your action and stored until automated deletion:

  • Name and URL of the retrieved file
  • Website from which access is made (referrer URL)

The data mentioned is processed by us for the following purposes: ensuring a smooth connection, ensuring convenient use of our website, and for evaluating system security and stability.

Your data will be deleted after the final processing of your request, unless there are legal retention obligations to the contrary.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. We distinguish between:

These cookies are essential for the operation of the website and cannot be disabled. They do not store any personal data.

Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we use analytics cookies that help us understand user behavior on our website and improve it. These cookies are only set after your explicit consent.

You can adjust your cookie settings at any time via our Cookie Settings page.
